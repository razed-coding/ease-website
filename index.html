How it works

Three steps to a clearer head

No sign-up. No cloud sync. Everything stays on your phone.

01
Tell us exactly what you drink

Add each drink in a session with its own ABV and volume. We don't judge — a 10% IPA and a 4% lager are different, and your plan should know that.

  • Every drink gets its own strength and price
  • Mix and match types — beer, wine, spirits, cocktails
  • Bar or home prices — auto-adjusted at 35%
02
Get a plan that actually fits you

Lessen builds a week-by-week reduction plan based on your real numbers — not a generic template. Each step is small enough to stick to.

  • Targets units — not just drink count
  • Choose full sobriety or moderate drinking
  • See your target date from day one
03
Log daily, watch the numbers move

Each evening, log how many you had. Your streak grows, your savings add up, and your unit count drops. Small wins compound fast.

  • Streak tracking — no broken chains
  • Real money saved, calculated daily
  • Health milestone timeline as you progress
